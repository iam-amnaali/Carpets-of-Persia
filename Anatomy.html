Main

Fringe

The exposed area of the warp strings, a part of the foundation’s structure, located on the top and bottom edges of the carpet.

Outer Secondary Border

The outer narrow band that frames the wide main border of the rug.

Main Border

The widest most prominent border that surrounds the central field of the carpet.

Inner Secondary Border

The narrow inner band that frames the field.

Corner Brackets or Spandrels

Decorative angular or curved areas repeated on all four corners of the field enclosed by the inner secondary border.

Field

The large central background area, often adorned with patterns, enclosed by the carpet borders.

Pendants

Decorative extensions of the central medallion.

Central Medallion

The prominent circular, oval or lobe shaped motif placed in the centre of the field.

Corner Articulations

Resolved Corner

Adjusted corner design that turns naturally and aligns correctly with both edges of the corner.

Un-resolved Corner

Corner design that continues past the edge without adjustment.
